Defining Data Analytics

Data Analytics is the systematic process of examining raw data to discover patterns, draw conclusions, and extract meaningful insights. It involves applying statistical and computational techniques, often to large and complex datasets.

Crucially, DA is about more than just crunching numbers; it's about understanding the story the data tells. This narrative helps individuals and organizations make informed, evidence-based decisions, leading to improved outcomes and strategic advantages.
